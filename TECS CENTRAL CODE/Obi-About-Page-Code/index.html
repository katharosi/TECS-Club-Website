About Us

General Meetings Monday, Wednesday, Friday at 2:30pm

We meet in room T206 in the Center of Technology Building. The Essex County College Club aims to promote a community of students who are seeking careers in STEM, or those who a simply just interested.

Every semseter, we meet, plan and come up with ideas to continously expand the club.

We are a community of liked-minded indivudals who are eager to succeed. We aim to help every person grow and feel confident in their tech skills once they graduate. Come join us, as we have dicussions about all things computer science. We hope to see you there!
